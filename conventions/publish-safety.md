# Publish Safety Rules

This repository is intended to be publishable.

Do not publish:

- private journals
- family, health, finance, or relationship context
- company-internal systems, incidents, meetings, team names, or unreleased work
- tokens, account IDs, private URLs, or local machine paths
- private Obsidian notes copied without review

Publishable notes may include:

- open-source project design
- publishable research
- generalized workflow patterns
- AI-native work principles
- decisions that are safe to share

Safety checks reduce mistakes, but they do not replace human review.
