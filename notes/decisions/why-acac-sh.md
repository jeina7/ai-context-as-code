# Why acac.sh

## Summary

The product name is `acac.sh`.
The old descriptive name, `AI Context as Code`, remains useful as a phrase for what the product does.
The public product should lead with a short domain-like name that fits developer tooling.

## Decision

Use `acac.sh` as the product name and public domain.
Keep `ai-context-as-code` as the repository name for now because it still explains the source project and avoids extra coordination cost.

## Why

`acac.sh` is short enough to remember and works as both brand and URL.

The `.sh` ending fits the developer-facing surface of the product. The system handles notes, rules, commands, scripts, generated metadata, and agent-readable configuration.

The old phrase still matters. The product treats context as a versioned, reviewable, testable artifact, but that phrase is now an explanation rather than the product name.
