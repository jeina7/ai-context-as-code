# AI-Native Expertise

## Summary

AI-native expertise is not just using stronger models.
The expert designs the workflow, context, verification, and human responsibility around AI output.
Useful AI systems reduce repeated context recovery and make judgment easier to apply.

## Principle

When AI can produce many plausible outputs, the main bottleneck moves from production to verification.

Strong AI-native work strengthens at least one of these:

- reliable source of truth
- explicit verification layer
- closed feedback loop
- captured intent and tradeoffs
- clear boundaries for what not to automate
- human responsibility for hard decisions

## Application

[[acac-sh-design]] exists to make durable context available to both humans and AI agents. It should not only increase note volume. It should make context easier to verify, reuse, and improve.
